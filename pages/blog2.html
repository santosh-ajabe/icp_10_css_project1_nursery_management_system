Top 5 Pet-Friendly Plants for Your Home

Explore the best plants that are safe for your furry friends.

Best Pet-Friendly Plants

Boston Fern

The Boston Fern is a lush and attractive pet-friendly plant. It’s easy to care for and can thrive in most indoor environments.

  • Great for humid environments.
  • Safe for pets, including rabbits.
  • Prefers indirect sunlight.

Spider Plant

Spider plants are not only easy to care for but also safe for both cats and dogs. They thrive in indirect sunlight and are great for beginner plant owners.

  • Air-purifying qualities.
  • Non-toxic to pets.
  • Thrives in low light.

Areca Palm

The Areca Palm is a popular houseplant that’s safe for pets. It can grow tall and adds a tropical vibe to your home.

  • Non-toxic to cats and dogs.
  • Requires bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Perfect for pet-friendly indoor spaces.

Calathea

Known for its beautiful foliage, the Calathea is also safe for pets. Its striking leaves can be a great addition to any room in your home.

  • Non-toxic to pets.
  • Requires moderate to low light.
  • Likes a humid environment.

Maranta (Prayer Plant)

The Maranta, also known as the Prayer Plant, is non-toxic to pets and offers beautiful patterned leaves that fold up at night, resembling prayer hands.

  • Safe for dogs and cats.
  • Needs bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Humidity-loving plant.
