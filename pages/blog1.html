How to Care for Plants in Every Season

Discover seasonal tips and tricks to keep your plants thriving all year round.

Seasonal Plant Care Guide

Spring

Spring is the season of growth and rejuvenation. Start by pruning, adding compost, and planting vibrant seasonal flowers like tulips and daffodils.

  • Water regularly as temperatures rise.
  • Fertilize soil to encourage growth.
  • Remove weeds to promote plant health.

Summer

Summer heat can be tough on plants. Water deeply and frequently to keep them hydrated. Mulching helps retain soil moisture.

  • Shade delicate plants during peak heat.
  • Deadhead flowers to promote new blooms.
  • Watch out for pests like aphids.

Autumn

Autumn is a time to prepare for the coming winter. Rake leaves and add them to your compost. Plant perennials and bulbs for spring blooms.

  • Reduce watering as temperatures drop.
  • Apply a layer of mulch to protect roots.
  • Harvest late-season vegetables and herbs.

Winter

Winter can be challenging for plants. Move sensitive plants indoors or cover them with frost cloth. Minimize watering to prevent over-saturation.

  • Ensure indoor plants get enough light.
  • Inspect for pests regularly.
  • Use a humidifier for indoor plants.
